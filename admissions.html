How to Apply

At Our Lady, we believe in a personal and thorough admissions process to ensure the best fit for your child.

Application Process

  • Collect an Application Form

    • Parents/guardians must visit the school in person to collect the official application form.
    Forms are not available online, via email or fax.

  • Complete the Form

    • Fill in all required sections clearly and accurarately

  • Submit the Form

    • Return the completed application form to the school in person.

  • Pay the Application Fee

    • A non-refundable application fee of R100 must be paid when submitting the form.

Important Notes
  • Applications will not be processed without the R100 fee.
  • Ensure you attach all required supporting documents (e.g birth certificate, latest school report).
  • Office Hours: Monday to Friday, 08:00 - 15:00
