About Our School

"In Christ We Believe, In God We Rejoice."

We are rooted in Catholic values and guided by faith, Our Lady is a nurturing all-girls school
dedicated to shaping young women from Grade 8 to 12 into confident, compassionate leaders.

We offer both day schooling at no cost and boarding facilities.
Only our boarders are required to pay boarding fees.

Here, every girl is seen, supported, and encouraged to grow in wisdom, faith, and service.
