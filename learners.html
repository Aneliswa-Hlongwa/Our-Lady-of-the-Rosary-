Our Students

At Our Lady, our students are at the heart of everything we do. We are proud to nurture a diverse and vibrant community of young women who are passionate about learning, leadership, and service.

Student Life at Our Lady

  • We offer a wide range of academic and extracurricular activities to enrich our students' experience.
  • Our students are encouraged to take on leadership roles and participate in community service.
  • We celebrate the achievements and talents of our students through various events and programs.
  • We provide a supportive and inclusive environment where every student can thrive.

Student Leadership

Our student leadership program empowers young women to develop their leadership skills and make a positive impact in their school and community. Through various roles and responsibilities, our student leaders learn the values of integrity, empathy, and service.

Student Council

The Student Council represents the student body and works closely with school administration to address student concerns and organize events.

Prefects

Club Leaders

Club Leaders organize and manage various student clubs, promoting interests and talents in areas such as arts, sports, and academics.

Student Achievements

We are proud of our students' accomplishments in academics, sports, arts, and community service. Our students consistently excel in national exams, win awards in competitions, and contribute positively to society through various initiatives.

  • Academic Excellence: Many of our students achieve top marks in national exams and receive scholarships for higher education.
  • Arts and Culture: Our students actively participate in cultural events, showcasing their talents in music, dance, and drama.
  • Community Service: Our students lead and participate in various community service projects, making a difference in the lives of others.
